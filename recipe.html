Fried Rice

Fried Rice is my absolute favorite recipe because it's flavorful, super easy to make, and it's a great way to use leftover rice while adding vegetables. I usually add chicken, shrimp, and of course, veggies.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of cooked white rice(day-old)
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables
  • 2 green onions
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • Add salt & pepper to your tasting

Directions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of veg. oil in a large pan on medium heat.
  2. Scramble the eggs in the pan and remove and sit aside when done.
  3. Add remaining oil and saute'the garlic for 30 seconds.
  4. Add the mixed veggies and cook them until tender.
  5. Stir in the cooked rice and mix well!
  6. Add the soy sauce, salt & pepper. Stir well.
  7. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and begin to mix.
  8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes and then add green onions.
  9. Serve hot and eat & enjoy!
